According to dietary guidelines for Americans, a typical meal should consists of following compositions of Protein, Carbohydrates and Fats:

  • Protein: 10-30% of calories
  • Carbohydrates: 45-65% of calories
  • Fat: 25-35% of calories

Having said that, people are not aware of healthy sources of Protein, carbs and most importantly fats. Therefore here are some healthy sources of these important ingredients:
